ImmerCom 2024

The 2nd ACM Workshop on Mobile Immersive Computing, Networking, and Systems

To be held in conjunction with ACM MobiCom 2024

November 18th, 2024, Washington, D.C., USA

The goal of ImmerCom is to build a collaborative community that advances research and development in immersive computing, networking, and systems. The travel grant is supported by the CoMIC project (https://comic-xr.github.io/) through the National Science Foundation (NSF) to sponsor participation in ImmerCom 2024.

To apply for a travel grant, please submit the following information in PDF files before October 31th, 2024 (AoE), via this application form:

  • 1. Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • 2. Research Statement (maximum 2 pages):
    • Discuss your research projects to date.
    • Explain how participation in ImmerCom 2024 will benefit your current and future research.
    • Specify your research topics for which CoMIC can serve as a testbed (if any).
    • Discribe your insights on the grand challenges of immersive computing, networking, and systems.
  • 3. Nomination Letter (maximum 1 page) from the advisor/supervisor (if you are a student):
    • Confirmation that the student is in good academic standing.
    • The suitability of the ImmerCom program to the student's research areas.
    • How the student will benefit from attending ImmerCom.
