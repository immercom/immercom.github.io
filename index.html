ImmerCom 2024

The 2nd ACM Workshop on Mobile Immersive Computing, Networking, and Systems

To be held in conjunction with ACM MobiCom 2024

November 18, 2024, Washington, D.C., USA

Welcome

The program committee is delighted to invite you to the 2nd ACM Workshop on Mobile Immersive Computing, Networking, and Systems (ImmerCom 2024). The workshop will be held on November 18, 2024, in conjunction with MobiCom 2024.

Please kindly note that in-person participation at this workshop is strongly encouraged, but remote presentations can be made available upon request by the authors of accepted papers.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission deadline: July 19th, 2024, AoE July 26th, 2024, AoE
  • Paper acceptance notification: August 12th, 2024, AoE August 19th, 2024, AoE
  • Camera-ready due: September 13, 2024, AoE
  • Workshop Date: November 18, 2024
