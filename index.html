ImmerCom 2023

The 1st ACM Workshop on Mobile Immersive Computing, Networking, and Systems

To be held in conjunction with ACM MobiCom 2023

October 6, 2023, Madrid, Spain

Welcome

The program committee is delighted to invite you to the 1st ACM Workshop on Mobile Immersive Computing, Networking, and Systems (ImmerCom 2023). The workshop will be held on the October 6, 2023, in conjunction with MobiCom 2023.

Please kindly note that in-person participation at this workshop is strongly encouraged, but remote presentations can be made available upon request by the authors of accepted papers.

Important Dates

  • Abstract registration deadline: June 12th, 2023, AoE
  • Paper submission deadline: June 19th, 2023, AoE
  • Paper acceptance notification: July 10th, 2023, AoE
  • Camera-ready due: August 11th, 2023, AoE
